# Japan stablecoin regulation — June 2026 update

## Headline change

Revised Payment Services Act (改正資金決済法) takes effect June 13, 2026. Yen-pegged stablecoins (JPYC) and major foreign-issued ones (USDC) become formally regulated assets distributable via licensed intermediaries.

## Why it matters

- Domestic exchanges can list USDC pairs without the prior trust-bank workaround
- Wallet providers gain a clear path to offering stablecoin custody
- Regulatory arbitrage with HK / Singapore narrows

- 5/15 FSA intermediary briefing — registration confirmed
- 6/13 effective date — coordinate listing announcements with comms team
